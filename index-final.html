Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Introvy.ai

How does the AI generate interview questions?

Our AI analyzes the job description, company information, and role requirements to create 3-5 tailored questions that help you showcase relevant skills and experience for that specific position.

Can I regenerate questions if I don't like them?

Yes! Free users get 1 regeneration per submission, while Pro users get 2 regenerations per submission. This helps manage AI costs while giving you flexibility.

What are the different difficulty levels?

We offer Easy (basic questions), Medium (standard professional questions), and Hard (advanced, challenging questions). Free users get Easy and Medium, while Pro users get access to all levels.

Do recruiters need to create an account?

No! Recruiters can post video job spotlights without any account creation. Just record your video, fill in the job details, pay $25, and you're live on our job wall.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade security, encrypt all data, and never share your personal information. You control who sees your video submissions.

Can I use Introvy for any type of job?

Yes! Our AI works with job descriptions from any industry - tech, healthcare, finance, retail, education, and more. The questions adapt to the specific role and requirements.
